Information

This is an open tournament. Anyone can register. You can register at our registration page. The registration fee will be $20. Please note that this is on a first-come first-serve basis and you are not guranteed a spot.

The tournament starts at 2pm and will last until 5pm.

The tournament will consist of 8 teams. Each team will consist of 5 people.

There will be 4 rounds and the winning team will split $500.
